Atomic Habits
James Clear
SUMMARY: A proven framework for improving every day through tiny, consistent changes.
LEARNINGS: Emphasized building robust systems rather than setting distant goals, allowing habits to compound naturally over time.
The Psychology of Money
Morgan Housel
SUMMARY: Timeless lessons on wealth, greed, and happiness, exploring the strange ways people think about and handle money.
LEARNINGS: Realized that financial success is less about hard science and what you know, and almost entirely about human behavior and managing emotions.
The Intelligent Investor
Benjamin Graham
SUMMARY: The definitive and foundational text on value investing, teaching how to analyze long-term stock value and minimize economic risks.
LEARNINGS: Internalized the concept of the "margin of safety" and the importance of ignoring short-term market noise in favor of deep, fundamental value.
A Random Walk Down Wall Street
Burton G. Malkiel
SUMMARY: A no-nonsense guide to investing that argues for the efficient-market hypothesis and the superiority of passive index funding.
LEARNINGS: Understood that broad-based index funds generally outperform actively managed portfolios over the long run, saving both time and management fees.
It Ends with Us
Colleen Hoover
SUMMARY: A powerful and deeply personal story about love, resilience, and the difficult choices involved in breaking cycles of abuse.
LEARNINGS: Gained profound empathy for people navigating complex, painful emotional situations and realized the immense strength required to make the right choice, even when it hurts.
November 9
Colleen Hoover
SUMMARY: An emotional novel about a writer and his unlikely muse who agree to meet on the same date every year for five consecutive years.
LEARNINGS: Explored themes of destiny, forgiveness, and the idea that true connection allows both individuals to independently grow into their own potential.
